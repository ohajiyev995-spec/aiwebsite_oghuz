About the project

UFC: Two Per Division is a fan-built mini-site that spotlights the top two contenders in every UFC division. It’s a quick-hit reference for stats, summaries, and notable fights without the noise.

Project blurb

This build started as a multi-page template and was reshaped into a UFC-focused hub. Each division is captured in a curated format, with fighter pages that expand on the story, style, and signature matchups that define their current run.

Everything is static, fast, and easy to maintain: update the roster data, re-run the generator, and every fighter page stays in sync.
